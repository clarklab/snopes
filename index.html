We work tirelessly to curb the spread of misinformation and fake news, and we couldn’t do it without you. Many of our readers ask how they can help support our work more directly. For those who can afford to donate, this page will serve as your resource to do so.

Before you consider contributing, please note the following:

Any amounts sent to Snopes.com are purely donations and are not a part of any offer to purchase securities or any other interest in Snopes.com (or its parent company).

All donations are non-refundable, and you will own no equity, debt, or other interest in Snopes.com (or its parent company) as a result of your donation.

Since Snopes.com (and its parent company) is not a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization, donations are not tax-deductible as charitable contributions, but all funds received will be allocated in their entirety to our operating expenses, legal fees, and the continuation of our overall mission to fight misinformation.
